Mardi Himal Trek – Hidden Gem of the Annapurna Region

Trip Introduction

The Mardi Himal Trek is a short yet incredibly scenic trek in the Annapurna region, offering breathtaking views of Machhapuchhre (Fishtail), Annapurna South, and Hiunchuli. Less crowded and peaceful, this trek is perfect for those seeking a quieter Himalayan experience with dramatic ridge walks and close-up mountain scenery.

Key Trip Facts

  • Trip Duration: 7–9 Days
  • Highest Elevation: ~4,500 m (Mardi Himal Base Camp)
  • Trek Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best Seasons: March–May & September–November
  • Start / End Point: Kathmandu / Pokhara
  • Accommodation: Tea houses & mountain lodges
  • Trek Style: Supported lodge trekking

Why Choose Mardi Himal?

  • Stunning close-up views of Machhapuchhre
  • Less crowded than Annapurna Base Camp
  • Beautiful ridge-line trekking
  • Perfect short trek from Pokhara
  • Great mix of forest, alpine terrain & panoramas

What You’ll Experience

The trail passes through lush rhododendron forests, alpine meadows, and narrow ridges with dramatic drops on either side. As you gain altitude, the landscapes open to spectacular snow-covered peaks that feel almost within reach.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival in Kathmandu

Arrival, hotel transfer, and trek briefing.

Day 2 — Drive or Fly to Pokhara

Scenic journey to Pokhara, the gateway to the Annapurna region.

Day 3 — Drive to Kande – Trek to Forest Camp

Trek begins through rhododendron forests and hill villages. (5–6 hrs)

Day 4 — Forest Camp to Low Camp

Gradual ascent through dense forest with mountain glimpses. (4–5 hrs)

Day 5 — Low Camp to High Camp

Ridge-line trekking with panoramic Himalayan views. (4–5 hrs)

Day 6 — High Camp to Mardi Himal Base Camp – Return to High Camp

Early morning hike to base camp for spectacular sunrise views. (6–7 hrs)

Day 7 — High Camp to Siding Village

Steep descent through alpine terrain to traditional village. (6 hrs)

Day 8 — Drive Back to Pokhara

Return drive to Pokhara for rest and relaxation.

Day 9 — Return to Kathmandu / Departure

Drive or flight back to Kathmandu.

Inclusions & Support

  • Experienced trekking guide
  • Porters for luggage
  • Tea-house accommodation
  • All meals during trekking
  • Ground transportation
  • Trekking permits & TIMS card

Trekking Tips

  • Start early for clear mountain views
  • Carry warm layers for high camp
  • Good hiking boots are essential
  • Weather can change quickly — be prepared

Who Is This Trek For?

The Mardi Himal Trek is ideal for trekkers who want a short yet rewarding Himalayan adventure with fewer crowds, dramatic scenery, and unforgettable mountain views — perfect for both beginners with fitness and experienced trekkers.
