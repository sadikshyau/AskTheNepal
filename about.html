Who We Are

Ask the Nepal Adventure is a locally owned and operated adventure travel company based in Nepal, specializing in trekking, hiking, cultural tours, and customized Himalayan experiences. Our team consists of experienced and licensed local guides with deep knowledge of Nepal’s mountains, trails, and culture. We focus on delivering safe, well-organized, and authentic journeys that connect travelers with the natural beauty and rich traditions of Nepal. From classic trekking routes to lesser-known destinations, we design trips to suit different interests, fitness levels, and budgets. Ask the Nepal Adventure is committed to responsible tourism, supporting local communities, and providing personalized service to make every journey memorable.

About Ask The Nepal Adventure

Founded by passionate mountaineers and travel experts, we specialize in trekking, expeditions, and cultural adventures across Nepal. Our deep connection with the Himalayas allows us to design journeys that are meaningful, responsible, and life-changing.
