Trip Introduction

Darjeeling, nestled in the foothills of the Eastern Himalayas, is famous for its stunning sunrise views of Kanchenjunga, aromatic tea gardens, and charming colonial heritage. Known as the "Queen of the Hills," this hill station offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and adventure activities. From the iconic Toy Train to vibrant Buddhist monasteries, Darjeeling captivates travelers with its timeless charm and panoramic mountain vistas.

What You'll Experience

Your journey begins with the magical sunrise at Tiger Hill, where Kanchenjunga turns golden in the first light. Explore aromatic tea estates, ride the charming Toy Train through misty hills, visit peaceful monasteries, and stroll through bustling markets. The cool mountain air, colonial-era charm, and warm local hospitality make Darjeeling an unforgettable Himalayan escape.
