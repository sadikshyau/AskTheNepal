Everest Three Passes Trek

The Everest Three Passes Trek is widely regarded as one of Nepal’s most epic high-altitude walking adventures. This ambitious loop links the classic Everest Base Camp trail with the serene Gokyo Valley and crosses three towering Himalayan passes — Renjo La, Cho La, and Kongma La. Along the way, trekkers traverse sweeping glaciers, glacial lakes, remote Sherpa villages, and dramatic ridgelines with panoramic views of world-class Himalayan giants.

Key Trip Facts

  • Duration: ~19–20 Days
  • Maximum Elevation: ~5,535 m (Kongma La Pass)
  • Difficulty Level: Very Challenging
  • Best Seasons: Spring (Mar–May) & Autumn (Sep–Nov)
  • Start / End: Kathmandu
  • Accommodation: Tea houses and lodges
  • Trek Style: Supported lodge trekking with guide and porter

Why Choose This Trek?

  • Complete Everest Experience: Walk classic sections like Namche Bazaar, Tengboche, and Everest Base Camp, and ascend Kala Patthar for sunrise views.
  • Gokyo Lake Wilderness: Explore the turquoise waters of the Gokyo Lakes, surrounded by striking glacial scenery.
  • Three Himalayan Passes: Cross Renjo La (5,360 m), Cho La (5,420 m), and Kongma La (5,535 m).
  • Cultural Depth: Encounter traditional Sherpa settlements and ancient trade route trails.
  • Remote and Less Crowded: Fewer trekkers in higher regions near the passes compared to the standard EBC trek.

What Makes It Special?

Crossing all three passes provides multiple world-class viewpoints and the full diversity of the Khumbu Himalayas — from subtropical forests to high-altitude rock and ice. Witness the Ngozumpa Glacier, sacred highland lakes, and panoramic vistas of Everest, Lhotse, Cho Oyu, and Makalu.

Detailed Daily Itinerary

Day 1:

Arrive in Kathmandu, transfer to hotel, rest, and overview briefing.

Day 2:

Fly to Lukla & trek to Phakding.

Day 3:

Phakding to Namche Bazaar.

Day 4:

Rest & acclimatize in Namche.

Day 5:

Namche to Tengboche.

Day 6:

Tengboche to Dingboche.

Day 7:

Acclimatization in Dingboche.

Day 8:

Dingboche to Chhukung.

Day 9:

Cross Kongma La Pass to Lobuche.

Day 10:

Lobuche to Everest Base Camp via Gorakshep, overnight in Gorakshep.

Day 11:

Kala Patthar sunrise hike, trek to Dzongla.

Day 12:

Dzongla to Thagnak via Cho La Pass.

Day 13:

Thagnak to Gokyo Village.

Day 14:

Gokyo Ri exploration & rest.

Day 15:

Gokyo to Marlung via Renjo La Pass.

Day 16:

Marlung to Namche Bazaar.

Day 17:

Namche to Lukla.

Day 18:

Fly back to Kathmandu.

Day 19:

Free day in Kathmandu for sightseeing or shopping.

Day 20:

Departure.

Trekking Notes & Tips

  • Altitude: Above 5,500 m, strenuous climbs and descents.
  • Acclimatization: Rest days are critical for safety.
  • Gear: Hiking poles, crampons or microspikes, layered clothing.
  • Weather: Rapid changes with snow, wind, and cold common.
